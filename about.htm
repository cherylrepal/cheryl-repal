Who Am I?

Hi! Everyone! My name is Cheryl.
I am a graphic designer and a volunteer Sunday school teacher.
The third child of Deolita Moncada and Pablito Repal.
I was born on December 15, 1981 in Masbate.
My mom is a great sewer, and our dad is the best carpenter in town.

When I was child I dreamt of becoming a civil engineer because of the influence our Papa made in me.
I remember when he renovated our house, me and my older sister was his labourers.
Through that I've learned and loved carpentry.
Also because of that, the belief that 'I can do anything' was formed inside of me.
I always thought that if other can do it, I can able to do it too and do it much better.
So I didn't ask for help from anyone regardless of the situation.

I was 9 years old when I first encountered God.
Our Sunday school teacher said God can be our friend.
And I want a friend like God, so I asked Him to be my bestfriend.

October 31, 1999 - I've met an accident. My life drastically change and it make a 180 degree turn.
I am caught by surprise and I didn't welcome it with open arm.
Because I don't want anyone to feel sorry for me, I wore a mask to hide what's I truly feel inside.

11 months our mom died.
She left us, me and my other three younger siblings, to the care of our Ate (older sister) Joy.
At a young age, she took the responsibility of raising us, sending us to school, paying bills and taking good care of us.
My Ate (older sister) is the first person I admire.

2003, my Ate Joy approached me and asked me if I wanted to teach children in our Sunday school.
But I declined her, because I thought I'm not fit for job.
I am not patient with kids. I easily get irritated when someone is so loud.
But God called me. And after more than a decade I'm still teaching kids.
He fully equipped me to do the job.

Through the years, God has been graciously showered me with so much blessing
He sent a lot of people to help me along the way.
He has shaped me to who I am today and is still shaping me.
Now I know that everything happened in my life is God's way of bringing me closer to Him.
And there's no other way that I can fully understand His plan for me if I had not gone through all of that.
The reason of my existence is God and God alone.
