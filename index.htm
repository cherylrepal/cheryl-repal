How has change impacted my life?

Change is inevitable. It is the one thing that never changes in the cosmos. Sometimes we reject change because we fear its implications, but we can't help it.

Change may bring about exciting new opportunities, but it also often brings in challenging new realities like the one I am now confronting.

The magnitude of the changes that have occurred in my life is hard for me to fathom. As infinite as possible, down to the absolute minimum. As a result, I went from being an unstoppable force to becoming a captive of my own constraints.

However, as I've matured, I've grown to appreciate the fact that, despite change's inevitable negative effects, it also provides us with opportunities to learn and grow.

Discover my interest, trainings I've completed, favorite music, and a brief summary of my life on this page. Also included are the technical skills and life lessons I've picked up through the years.
